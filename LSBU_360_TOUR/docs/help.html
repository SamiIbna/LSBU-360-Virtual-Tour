Help & Controls

How to use the tour on desktop, mobile, and Meta Quest.

Desktop / Laptop
PC
Mouse
  • Click + drag to look around.
  • Press Play to start video because browsers often block autoplay.
  • Use tabs to switch stops, and use Next or Prev to navigate.
Mobile
MOB
Touch
  • Drag to pan.
  • Tap Play to start.
  • Use the Photo Page for still 360 photos.
Meta Quest
VR
Headset workflow
  • Open in Quest Browser and press Enter VR.
  • Use the floating in-scene panel with ray and trigger.
